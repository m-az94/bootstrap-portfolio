About Me

My Name is Maria Azam. I was born in Fredricton, New Brunswick, and grew up in the suburbs of Toronto. I grew up in the early 2000s, a time when very few people could use the internet, let alone understood how it works. Luckily for me, I recieved early exposure to web design from my father. From a young age, I used my extremely limited knowledge to make webpages about everything I could think of. I'm excited to go back to my roots and see what I could accomplish as a professional web developer. Currently, I am an aspiring web developer, a highschool STEM tutor and, I also barista on the side.
